Meet Our Clinical Team

Our counselors and specialists bring over 50 years of combined experience in addiction recovery, mental health, and trauma. Each one is here because they believe recovery is possible — for everyone.

Who you'll work with

Clinicians who understand recovery from the inside

Every member of our team is clinically trained and credentialed. Many are in personal recovery themselves — which means they bring something textbooks can't teach. They understand addiction and mental health from the inside, and that changes everything about how they show up for clients.

LPC · CAADC

Britt Parramore

Clinical Director

As Clinical Director, Britt brings both the credentials and the conviction that real recovery requires treating the whole person. A Licensed Professional Counselor and Certified Advanced Alcohol and Drug Counselor, he leads the clinical team with a commitment to evidence-based care paired with genuine human connection.

CADCII · CCDS

Judy Relf

Addiction Counselor & Co-occurring Disorders Specialist

Judy's credentials as a Certified Alcohol and Drug Counselor II and Certified Co-occurring Disorders Specialist reflect her expertise in treating addiction alongside mental health challenges. She brings a warm, direct approach that helps clients feel seen without feeling judged.

CIT · CARES · CPS-AD

Adam E. Miller

Peer Support Specialist & Recovery Counselor

Adam brings lived experience into the clinical room, serving as a Certified Peer Specialist in Addiction. His certifications in crisis intervention and addiction recovery support give clients someone who understands what recovery actually demands — not just in theory.

EMDR Specialist

Dr. Michael Reece Carpenter

Trauma & EMDR Specialist

Dr. Carpenter specializes in Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR), one of the most effective evidence-based approaches to trauma treatment. He works with clients whose addiction and mental health challenges are rooted in past trauma — helping them process what's been stuck for years.

CPS

Scott Brown

Certified Peer Specialist

Scott brings the power of shared experience to his work as a Certified Peer Specialist. In personal recovery himself, he walks alongside clients not as someone who has read about the road — but as someone who has traveled it. His presence in the clinical team embodies what makes Pathlight different.
