Signs anxiety therapy can help

If this sounds like you, we can help

→Constant worry that you can't switch off, even when things are fine
→Avoidance of situations, people, or responsibilities because of fear
→Physical symptoms — tight chest, upset stomach, tension — without a clear medical cause
→Sleep disruption from racing thoughts or dread about the next day
→Panic attacks or waves of intense, sudden fear
→Anxiety that's affecting your work, your relationships, or your daily quality of life
