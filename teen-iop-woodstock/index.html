For parents

You're not overreacting. And this isn't your fault.

Most parents come to us feeling two things at once: terrified about what's happening with their teenager, and guilty that it got this far. You're not overreacting if your gut is telling you something is seriously wrong. And this isn't a reflection of your parenting.

Teenagers who need this level of support often have something else going on beneath the surface — anxiety, depression, trauma, or social pain they don't have words for. Substance use became a way to cope. Our job is to help your teenager find better ways, in a program that actually understands the adolescent brain and experience.

We also work with families throughout the program. Because recovery doesn't happen in isolation — and you need support too.

What you might be seeing

Signs your teenager may need more than talk therapy

→Alcohol or drug use that's escalating or becoming more secretive despite conversations about it
→Grades have dropped significantly, or they've stopped caring about school entirely
→You've lost access to them — the real them — behind a wall of anger, silence, or distance
→Friends have changed, old friendships have disappeared, or they've become isolated
→Mood swings that are more extreme than normal teen behavior — rage, crashes, complete withdrawal
→Weekly therapy sessions haven't moved the needle — you need more structure and consistency

Why this program is different

Built for teenagers. Not adapted from adults.

There's a big difference between a teen IOP and an adult program with younger clients mixed in. Ours is purpose-built for adolescents — the only one like it in Northern Georgia.

Adolescent-specific clinical approach

Teenagers' brains work differently. Our counselors are trained in adolescent development and use approaches designed for where teens actually are — not where adults are.

Family involvement

We work with you throughout the program. Your teenager's recovery happens in your home, with your family — so you're part of the process, not left on the outside of it.

The only teen IOP in North Georgia

Families across Cherokee, Cobb, Forsyth, Fulton, and surrounding counties come to us because there is no other program like this nearby. Your teenager doesn't have to leave the region to get serious help.

Structured around school

We work to fit the program around school schedules and other commitments so your teenager's education continues during treatment.

Group and individual support

Teens often open up in peer group settings in ways they won't with adults alone. Our group component brings teens together with others who understand — without shame.

Faith-integrated option

For families who want faith as part of their teenager's recovery, we can incorporate that meaningfully into the work.
