Is IOP right for you?

You need more than weekly therapy, but you can't step out of your life.

You've probably thought about residential treatment. Maybe you've even done some research. But the reality of your job, your family, your mortgage — it's not that simple to just leave.

That's exactly who our Intensive Outpatient Program was designed for. You get the structure and depth of a serious recovery program while keeping your life running. Our adult IOP is ASAM Level 2.1 certified — which means it meets the national standard for meaningful, substantive addiction treatment.

It's not easy. It requires real commitment. But it works — and you can do it while still showing up for your life.

Signs that IOP might be the right level of support

→You've stopped and started multiple times and weekly therapy hasn't been enough to sustain it
→Alcohol or drug use has started affecting your work, your relationships, or your health in ways you can't ignore
→You feel like you need more structure and accountability than one session a week provides
→You want to address the root causes — not just the behavior — and you're willing to do the work
→You're not in immediate medical danger, but you know what you're dealing with is serious
→You've looked at residential programs and can't make them work logistically or financially

What to expect

How the IOP actually works

4-month structured program

The program runs approximately four months and combines group counseling with individual sessions. Structure and consistency are what make it work.

Group and individual sessions

You'll work in both small group settings and one-on-one with your counselor. The group component is often where some of the most powerful shifts happen.

Whole-person approach

We look at the biological, psychological, social, and spiritual dimensions of your recovery — because addiction never operates in just one area of your life.

Faith integration available

For clients who want it, we weave in biblically sound principles alongside clinical tools. Your faith can be part of your recovery if you want it to be.

ASAM Level 2.1 certified

Our IOP meets national addiction treatment standards — meaning you're getting real, substantive care, not a watered-down program.

You go home every day

Unlike residential treatment, you sleep in your own bed, stay connected to your family, and keep your responsibilities. The program fits into your life — not the other way around.
