You didn't choose what happened to you

Trauma doesn't always look like what you think it does.

A lot of people don't think of themselves as having "trauma" — because what happened to them doesn't match the dramatic version they've seen portrayed. But trauma is anything that overwhelmed your ability to cope at the time it happened, and left a mark on how you see yourself and the world.

It shows up as hypervigilance, numbness, intrusive memories, or a sense that you're never truly safe — even in situations that should feel normal. It shows up in relationships, in how you respond to stress, and in the quiet background hum of something not being right.

At Pathlight Counseling in Woodstock, GA, our trauma-informed therapists — including EMDR specialist Dr. Michael Carpenter — help you process the experiences that are still affecting you, without requiring you to relive them unnecessarily.

Signs trauma may be affecting you

Trauma can look like many things

→Flashbacks, intrusive memories, or nightmares about a past event
→Feeling emotionally numb, disconnected, or "not really here"
→Hypervigilance — always scanning for danger, can't fully relax
→Avoiding people, places, or situations that remind you of what happened
→Difficulty trusting others or feeling safe in relationships
→Substance use that started as a way to cope with something painful

Our approach to trauma

How we treat trauma at Pathlight

EMDR Therapy

Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR) is one of the most research-supported treatments for trauma. Dr. Michael Carpenter, our EMDR specialist, helps clients process traumatic memories so they lose their emotional charge. Learn more on our EMDR therapy page.

Trauma-informed care throughout

Every member of our team is trained to work with trauma sensitively. You won't be pushed to talk about things before you're ready — we build safety first.

Addiction and trauma together

Trauma and substance use often travel together — one feeding the other. We treat both, which is why our IOP and individual counseling programs are built around this connection.
