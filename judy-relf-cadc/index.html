About Judy

Judy Relf is a Certified Alcohol and Drug Counselor II (CADCII) and Certified Co-occurring Disorders Specialist (CCDS) at Pathlight Counseling in Woodstock, GA. Her credentials reflect a deep expertise in treating addiction not as an isolated problem, but as something that almost always travels with other mental health challenges — anxiety, depression, trauma, and more.

What clients notice about Judy first is her warmth. She creates a space where people feel genuinely seen without feeling judged — which matters enormously for clients who've spent years carrying shame about their struggles. Her direct, no-nonsense approach helps clients cut through the noise and actually move forward.

Treating the Whole Picture

Many people who struggle with addiction also struggle with anxiety, depression, or unresolved trauma. These issues feed each other — and treating only the addiction while ignoring the rest rarely leads to lasting change. As a Certified Co-occurring Disorders Specialist, Judy is trained to hold both pieces at once.

That integrated approach is exactly what many clients have been missing. They've tried programs that treated the addiction and sent them home — only to find that the anxiety or the old grief or the unprocessed trauma was still there, waiting. Judy addresses all of it.

A Space That Feels Safe

People who come to Judy for the first time often describe feeling nervous — about what they'll have to say, what they'll be judged for, what the process will be like. Most of them say that nervousness fades faster than they expected. Judy has a way of making the conversation feel natural, and of asking the right questions without it feeling like an interrogation.

That matters in addiction recovery. The shame that keeps people stuck is often just as powerful as the substance itself. Judy helps dismantle it.
