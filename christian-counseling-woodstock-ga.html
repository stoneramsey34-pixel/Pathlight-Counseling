Faith and clinical care — together

You want a counselor who understands that faith is part of the healing.

For many people, faith isn't just something they practice on Sundays — it's the framework through which they understand themselves, their relationships, and their struggles. Finding a counselor who respects that — and knows how to work with it rather than around it — makes all the difference.

At Pathlight Counseling in Woodstock, GA, we integrate Christian principles into our clinical work for clients who want that. This isn't a watered-down faith conversation, nor a clinical approach that ignores your beliefs. It's a genuine integration of sound theology and evidence-based therapy — held by clinicians who take both seriously.

And if you prefer a strictly secular approach, we meet you there too. Your comfort and your preferences guide how we work together.

What Christian counseling looks like at Pathlight

Faith-integrated care that doesn't compromise on either side

Biblically grounded, not preachy

We don't lecture. We walk alongside you — bringing biblical wisdom into the room when it's relevant, in a way that feels natural and helpful rather than heavy-handed.

Evidence-based clinical methods

Faith integration doesn't replace proven clinical approaches — it works alongside them. Our counselors are trained in CBT, trauma-informed care, and other research-backed methods.

For individuals, couples, and families

We offer Christian counseling for individuals working through personal struggles, couples navigating marriage, and families seeking healing together.

Rooted in the Woodstock community

We understand the faith landscape of Cherokee County. Our practice is part of this community — and we see clients from churches, families, and ministry backgrounds across the region.
