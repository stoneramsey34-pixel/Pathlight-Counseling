Every marriage hits hard seasons. The question is whether you face them together.

You didn't build a life together to end up feeling like strangers. But between work, kids, finances, and the weight of daily life — it's easy for a marriage to quietly drift apart before either partner fully realizes it's happening.

At Pathlight Counseling, just minutes from Canton in Woodstock, we work with couples who are ready to do something about it. Our counselors help you break the patterns keeping you stuck, rebuild real communication, and rediscover what the marriage is actually supposed to be.

For couples whose faith is part of the foundation, we integrate Christian principles alongside evidence-based couples work. This isn't just therapy — it's a conversation grounded in what you believe about marriage.
