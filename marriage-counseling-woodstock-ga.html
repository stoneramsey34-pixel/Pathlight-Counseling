For couples who want more

Every marriage goes through hard seasons. This one doesn't have to define yours.

You didn't get married to feel like roommates. But somewhere along the way, the connection frayed — and now the same arguments keep circling, or the silence has grown louder than the words. It happens to couples who love each other deeply.

At Pathlight Counseling in Woodstock, GA, we work with couples who are ready to do something about it. Our counselors help you break the patterns that keep you stuck, rebuild the kind of communication that actually works, and rediscover what brought you together in the first place.

For couples who want their faith to be part of the process, we integrate Christian principles alongside proven clinical approaches. Marriage was designed to thrive — and we believe it can.

Common reasons couples come in

You might be here because…

→The same arguments keep happening and never actually get resolved
→You feel more like co-parents or roommates than partners
→Trust was broken — by an affair, a secret, or a pattern of dishonesty
→You're struggling to talk without it turning into a fight
→Life stress — finances, kids, work, grief — is pulling you apart instead of together
→One or both of you has considered whether this marriage can survive
