For individuals

You don't have to be in crisis to deserve support.

A lot of people wait until things get really bad before they call a counselor. They convince themselves they should be able to handle it — that other people have it worse, that they just need to push through. Maybe that sounds like you.

You don't have to be in crisis to benefit from counseling. If something in your life isn't working — anxiety that won't quit, a sadness that's been sitting on your chest, a pattern you keep repeating — those are exactly the things counseling is for.

One-on-one sessions give you a private, judgment-free space to talk through what's really going on, understand the patterns beneath the surface, and start making actual changes — not just coping.

Signs that individual counseling might help

→Anxiety that makes it hard to be present — constant worry, overthinking, or dread you can't shake
→Depression that's dulled your interest in things that used to matter to you
→Alcohol or substance use that's become a way to manage stress or emotions
→Patterns in relationships or behavior that you recognize but can't seem to change on your own
→A major life change — divorce, grief, job loss — that you haven't fully processed
→Just feeling stuck. Like you're going through the motions but not actually living.

What counseling at Pathlight looks like

Understanding the root cause

Symptoms like anxiety or depression usually have something underneath them. Counseling helps you understand what's actually driving the patterns — not just manage the symptoms.

Practical tools, not just talking

You'll learn actual skills — ways of thinking, responding, and relating — that you can use in your daily life. This isn't just venting. It's work that produces change.

Faith-integrated if you want it

For clients who want their faith to be part of the conversation, we can integrate that meaningfully. We believe spiritual health and mental health aren't separate things.

Positive Psychology approach

We look at your strengths and what's working in your life — not just what's broken. Building from what's good in you is often the most direct path to change.

For adults and teens

We work with both adult individuals and teenagers in one-on-one counseling. If you're seeking counseling for a teen alongside the IOP, we can coordinate both.
