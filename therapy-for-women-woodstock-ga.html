A space built for you

You spend so much time showing up for others. This is where you show up for yourself.

Women often come to therapy after years of quietly managing everything — their work, their relationships, their families, their emotions. By the time they call, they've usually been struggling longer than they want to admit.

At Pathlight Counseling in Woodstock, GA, we see this all the time. Women who are smart, capable, and deeply compassionate — and who have put their own needs last for so long that they've almost forgotten what it felt like to not be exhausted.

Our counselors offer a warm, shame-free space to talk about what's actually going on: the anxiety, the grief, the relationship patterns, the way past experiences are still shaping your present. Whatever brought you here, you don't have to figure it out alone.

→Anxiety that follows you everywhere — even when there's no clear reason for it
