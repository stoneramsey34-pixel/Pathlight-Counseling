What sets us apart

Why people choose Pathlight

We're not the right fit for everyone — but for the right person, this is exactly what they've been looking for.

The only teen IOP in North Georgia

Built specifically for teenagers — not borrowed from an adult model. Families come from across the region because there's nothing else like it nearby.

Faith-integrated care

Clinical training and biblically sound principles — together. For many people in recovery, faith is part of the foundation they're rebuilding on.

Our clinicians are in personal recovery

Many of our staff are in recovery themselves. They bring both the credentials and the lived experience — which means they understand your struggle in a way textbooks can't teach.

50+ years combined experience

Deep experience with addiction, trauma, and mental health — not just credentials. Our team has walked alongside people through real recovery.

ASAM Level 2.1 certified IOP

National-standard outpatient treatment intensity. You're getting substantive care, not a watered-down program.

Free veteran services

Fully grant-funded through COSSUP — free to veterans and all family members. Specialized support for service members who deserve care that understands their service.

Serving all of North Georgia

Woodstock-based, serving Cherokee County, Cobb, Forsyth, Pickens, Dawson, North Fulton, and surrounding areas.
